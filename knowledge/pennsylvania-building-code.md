**Base Code**: IBC 2018 with Pennsylvania amendments
**Authority**: Pennsylvania Department of Labor & Industry, Bureau of Occupational & Industrial Safety

## Residential Code — SPRINKLER REQUIREMENT

**Pennsylvania requires fire sprinklers in ALL new one- and two-family dwellings** (since 2004) — one of very few states with this mandate.

- **System type**: NFPA 13D
- **No exceptions**: Applies statewide regardless of jurisdiction
- **Design**: Full coverage per NFPA 13D — bathrooms, closets, attached garages

### Other Key Amendments
- **Radon**: Zone 1 (central/northeast PA — Reading Prong has highest radon in US); Zone 2 elsewhere — active radon mitigation system mandatory in Zone 1
- **Frost depth**: 36" statewide
- **Snow**: Lake-effect areas northwest PA (Erie) — 40-50 psf; rest 25-35 psf

## Seismic / Wind / Natural Hazards

### Seismic
- Statewide SDC A-B — low risk
- Standard provisions apply

### Wind
- Statewide: 90-100 mph design
- No hurricane exposure (inland state)
- Tornado: Moderate risk — 15-20 per year; no enhanced provisions

### Snow
| Region | Ground Snow Load |
|--------|----------------|
| Philadelphia metro | 25 psf |
| Pittsburgh | 25-30 psf |
| Erie (lake-effect) | 40-50 psf |
| Pocono Mountains | 40-50 psf |
| Laurel Highlands | 35-45 psf |

### Radon (Critical)
- **Reading Prong**: Montgomery, Berks, Bucks, Northampton counties — very high (some of highest in US)
- **Zone 1**: Central PA, northeastern PA — radon mitigation mandatory
- **Active system**: Required in Zone 1; passive in Zone 2

### Flooding
- FEMA flood zones along Susquehanna, Delaware, Allegheny rivers
- Post-Agnes (1972) and Ivan/Lee events — significant floodplain regulation

## Notable State Amendments
- **Sprinklers mandatory**: Critical differentiator — always include NFPA 13D in residential
- **Philadelphia local code**: Philadelphia has own code adoption (currently IBC 2018); verify
- **Pittsburgh**: Allegheny County local amendments
- **UCC Municipal opt-out**: Some small municipalities opted out of UCC — verify AHJ

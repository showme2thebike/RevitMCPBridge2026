## Residential Code

**Adopted**: NC Residential Code, based on IRC 2018

### Key Amendments
- **Sprinklers**: Not required for 1-2 family statewide
- **Coastal**: CAMA setbacks (100' from mean high water for major developments)
- **Wind**: Coastal counties — enhanced framing, straps, impact glazing
- **Termite**: Required throughout state — significant subterranean termite pressure
- **Moisture**: Vapor barrier required; crawl space encapsulation recommended Piedmont/Coast

## Seismic / Wind / Natural Hazards

### Wind / Hurricane
| Region | Design Wind Speed | Notes |
|--------|-----------------|-------|
| Outer Banks / Cape Hatteras | 140-150 mph | Extreme exposure |
| Wilmington / Crystal Coast | 130-140 mph | Wind-borne debris region |
| Morehead City area | 130 mph | |
| Raleigh / Charlotte | 100-105 mph | Standard |
| Mountains (Asheville) | 100 mph | |

- **CAMA (Coastal Area Management Act)**: 100' setback from mean high water — AEC permit required
- **Wind-borne debris region**: Coastal counties — impact glazing or shutters required
- **Hurricane straps**: Required coastal framing — continuous load path

### Seismic
- Statewide SDC A-B — low risk
- Standard provisions apply

### Tornado
- Moderate risk — Piedmont and coastal plain
- No enhanced statewide tornado provisions

### Flooding
- Coastal: V/AE zones (FEMA) — storm surge significant
- Inland: 100/500-year floodplains along Neuse, Cape Fear, Tar rivers
- Post-Floyd/Matthew/Dorian: Enhanced FEMA floodplain management in eastern NC

## Notable State Amendments
- **CAMA**: Coastal Area Management Act — separate permit process for oceanfront/estuarine
- **Mountain Land Development**: Slope stability, steep slope regulations in mountain counties
- **Charlotte/Raleigh**: Local amendments; verify with city building departments
- **Manufactured housing**: NCDMV regulates separately

## Quick Checklist - New Residential
- [ ] Climate zone (3A Piedmont/coast / 4A mountains)
- [ ] Coastal? CAMA permit required (within 75-100' of water)
- [ ] Wind zone — impact glazing for coastal counties
- [ ] Hurricane straps — coastal framing
- [ ] Termite protection specified
- [ ] Flood zone (FEMA FIRM)
- [ ] Crawl space — vapor barrier / encapsulation

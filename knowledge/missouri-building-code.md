## Residential Code

**Status**: Missouri has **no statewide residential building code**. Local adoption varies widely. Many rural areas have no residential code enforcement.

### Key Considerations
- **Kansas City and St. Louis metro**: Strong local codes enforced
- **Rural Missouri**: Many counties have no residential code; minimal oversight
- **Tornado**: Despite extreme risk, no statewide enhanced construction requirements
- **New Madrid seismic**: Southeast MO (Bootheel) — SDC C-D; requires careful attention
- **Radon**: Zone 1 in northern/central MO; Zone 2-3 elsewhere
- **Frost depth**: 30-36" Kansas City/St. Louis; 24" Bootheel/southeast

## Seismic / Wind / Natural Hazards

### Seismic (Southeast Missouri is Critical)
| Region | SDC | Notes |
|--------|-----|-------|
| Kansas City | A | Very low risk |
| St. Louis | A-B | Low risk |
| Cape Girardeau / Poplar Bluff | B-C | New Madrid proximity |
| Bootheel (New Madrid, Pemiscot) | C-D | **New Madrid Seismic Zone origin** |

- **New Madrid Seismic Zone (NMSZ)**: Missouri is the origin state of the zone — 1811-1812 sequence (M7.5-8.0) changed the course of the Mississippi River
- **Bootheel region**: SDC C-D; enhanced seismic design required for most commercial structures
- **St. Louis**: Moderate risk but significant unreinforced masonry older building stock
- Special inspections required SDC C+

### Tornado (Extreme Risk)
- **2011 Joplin EF5**: Killed 161 — deadliest single tornado in US history since modern records
- **1925 Tri-State Tornado**: F5 — killed 695 across MO/IL/IN — deadliest US tornado ever
- Missouri averages 35+ tornadoes/year
- Highest risk: central and southern MO (Joplin, Springfield, Cape Girardeau corridors)
- **No statewide enhanced construction provisions**

## Notable State Amendments
- **No residential code**: Largest state without statewide residential code — verify local AHJ completely
- **Kansas City**: Jackson County + city both enforce; strong local codes
- **St. Louis City**: Separate from St. Louis County; older building stock
- **Joplin**: Post-2011 tornado Joplin has strong local safe room encouragement and awareness
- **Bootheel**: New Madrid seismic risk widely underappreciated in region

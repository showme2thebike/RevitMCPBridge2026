## Residential Code

**Adopted**: Georgia Residential Code based on IRC 2018
**Authority**: Georgia DCA — local jurisdictions may locally amend

### Key Amendments
- **Sprinklers**: Not required for 1-2 family statewide
- **Termite protection**: Required statewide — Georgia has significant termite pressure (Formosan and eastern subterranean)
- **Crawl space**: Encapsulated crawl space common due to humidity; vapor barrier required
- **Hurricane straps**: Required coastal counties — Chatham, Bryan, Liberty, McIntosh, Glynn, Camden
- **Flood**: FEMA zones — coastal and river floodplains significant

## Seismic / Wind / Natural Hazards

### Seismic
- **Statewide**: SDC A-B — low seismic risk
- **Northern Georgia**: Slightly higher due to proximity to Eastern Tennessee Seismic Zone
- Standard IBC seismic provisions apply; no enhanced requirements

### Wind / Hurricane
| Region | Design Wind Speed | Notes |
|--------|-----------------|-------|
| Coastal (Savannah/Brunswick) | 130-140 mph | Wind-borne debris region |
| 1 mile inland from coast | 130 mph | |
| Atlanta metro | 100-105 mph | Standard design |
| North Georgia | 100 mph | |

- **Wind-borne debris region**: Within 1 mile of coast where V ≥ 130 mph
- **Impact glazing**: Required in wind-borne debris regions
- **Hurricane shutters**: Alternative to impact glazing

### Tornado
- Georgia experiences significant tornado activity
- March-May primary season; second peak October-November
- North Georgia (Atlanta corridor) highest in-state risk
- Standard construction — no specific enhanced tornado provisions statewide

### Flooding
- FEMA flood zones per local FIRMs
- Coastal Zone Management: FEMA V/AE zones along coast and tidal areas
- River floodplains: Chattahoochee, Savannah, Altamaha, Flint rivers
- CDBG-DR funded projects: Enhanced flood standards may apply

## Notable State Amendments

- **Termite**: Very high pressure — include physical barriers AND soil treatment
- **Humidity/moisture**: Encapsulated crawl space strongly recommended in Zones 2A-3A
- **Atlanta local amendments**: City of Atlanta has some local code amendments; verify with City
- **HERO Act**: Georgia High-Efficiency Residential Ordinance — local option for stricter energy
- **Historic Savannah**: Extensive historic district regulation — verify HPO requirements

## Residential Code

**Adopted**: Kentucky Residential Code, based on IRC 2018

### Key Amendments
- **Sprinklers**: Not required statewide for 1-2 family
- **Seismic**: Western KY (Paducah/McCracken County) — New Madrid Seismic Zone — SDC C; enhanced requirements
- **Radon**: Zone 1 (eastern and central KY — Appalachian region, Bluegrass karst); passive system required in high-risk counties; strongly recommended statewide
- **Tornado**: High risk statewide — 2021 western KY outbreak devastating
- **Frost depth**: 24-30" Louisville; 18" Paducah
- **Coal mine subsidence**: Eastern KY — special foundation considerations in mined areas

## Seismic / Wind / Natural Hazards

### Seismic (Western Kentucky is Critical)
| Region | SDC | Notes |
|--------|-----|-------|
| Eastern KY (Appalachian) | A | Very low risk |
| Lexington / Frankfort | A | Low risk |
| Louisville | A-B | Low risk |
| Western KY (Paducah area) | C | New Madrid Seismic Zone proximity |

- **New Madrid Seismic Zone**: Paducah is within the highest-risk area outside the New Madrid epicentral zone
- **Jackson Purchase region**: Western tip of KY — M7+ scenario possible; unreinforced masonry at significant risk
- Special inspections required SDC C

### Tornado
- **2021 Western Kentucky Tornado Outbreak (December 10-11)**: EF4 Mayfield tornado traveled 227 miles — deadliest single December tornado in US history; 77 killed in KY
- Kentucky averages 15-20 tornadoes/year; severe events possible any season
- No statewide enhanced tornado construction requirements

### Coal Mine Subsidence (Eastern Kentucky)
- Extensive underground coal mining history in eastern KY
- **Foundation design**: Shallow foundations at risk in mined areas; consult geotechnical report
- Mining maps available from Kentucky Division of Mine Permits

## Notable State Amendments
- **Louisville**: Jefferson County has local code enforcement; Louisville Metro Government
- **Lexington**: Fayette County strong enforcement; horse farm agricultural context
- **Paducah**: Western KY — seismic provisions SDC C; verify local AHJ awareness
- **Eastern KY**: Appalachian coalfield counties — mine subsidence, radon, steep-slope construction
- **KY Energy Efficiency programs**: Duke Energy KY, LG&E/KU incentives for above-code performance

- [ ] Climate zone (3A far western / 4A most of KY)
